Our Story: From Humble Beginnings to a Culinary Landmark

At Totot, our story is a testament to perseverance, passion, and the rich tapestry of Ethiopian culture. It all began with Tenkir Teni, a shoeshine boy in Addis Ababa with an unyielding dream. Through sheer hard work and determination, Tenkir transformed his humble beginnings into a thriving restaurant venture. Established in 2001 E.C. (Ethiopian Calendar), Totot—a name derived from the Gurage language meaning "let's work"—embodies the spirit of effort and dedication that built it.
